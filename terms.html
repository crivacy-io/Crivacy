Terms of Service

Last updated: February 2025

Crivacy operates a shared KYC credential layer for Canton Network applications. These terms describe what the service does, what users are responsible for, and what to expect during early access.

1. What the Service Is

Crivacy helps users complete KYC once and reuse a credential across integrated Canton apps. Sumsub performs verification, and Crivacy manages credential status and proof outcome handling for reuse across the network.

2. User Responsibilities

Users are responsible for providing accurate information during verification and using the service lawfully. If information is false or misleading, credential issuance or access may be rejected, revoked, or limited.

3. What a Credential Means

A Crivacy credential means a verification outcome exists and can be checked by integrated apps. Credential availability across different applications depends on partner integrations and may vary by app. It does not guarantee access to every app, and each app may apply its own eligibility, policy, or compliance rules.

4. Compliance

Crivacy does not make compliance decisions on behalf of applications. Each integrated application independently determines its own eligibility and regulatory requirements.

5. Service Changes

Crivacy may modify, suspend, or update features, flows, integrations, or requirements over time as the product and network evolve.

6. Early Access

Crivacy is in an early access stage. Some features may be incomplete, change over time, or become temporarily unavailable while the service is being improved.

7. Contact

For service questions or access requests, contact hello@crivacy.io.
