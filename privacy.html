Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Crivacy operates a shared KYC credential layer for the Canton Network. Crivacy acts as a credential orchestration layer. Identity verification data is processed by the verification provider (Sumsub). Crivacy receives only verification outcomes necessary to issue and manage credentials. Crivacy is designed to separate identity verification from on-ledger credential usage, minimizing data exposure.

1. Data Collection (KYC)

To complete KYC, identity verification data is collected through the verification flow. This may include identity document images, selfie or liveness checks, and basic profile information required by the KYC provider.

2. Processing via Sumsub

Crivacy uses Sumsub to run identity verification. Sumsub processes KYC data and returns a verification outcome to Crivacy so a credential can be issued or updated.

3. Raw Data and Deletion

Crivacy does not store raw KYC documents on Canton. Raw verification data is handled through the KYC provider flow and is not written to Canton.

4. What Is Written to Canton

Crivacy writes only credential proof status information to Canton. This may include verification status, timestamps, validity period, and a proof hash or reference identifier. Personal KYC documents and sensitive raw identity data are not stored on-ledger.

5. User Rights (Revoke)

  • Users may request credential revocation where available. Changes to credential status are reflected to supporting integrated apps via Canton.
  • Users may request deletion of applicable off-ledger records, subject to legal and compliance obligations.

6. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact hello@crivacy.io.
