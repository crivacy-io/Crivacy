About Crivacy

Shared KYC credential infrastructure for the Canton Network.

Crivacy lets users complete KYC once and reuse a credential across Canton applications. Sumsub performs verification off-ledger, while Crivacy writes only credential status and proof metadata to Canton.

How Crivacy Works

Current implementation and product logic in plain language.

Step 1

Verify Once

A user completes KYC through Sumsub. Documents are verified off-ledger and are not written to Canton.

Step 2

Issue Credential

Crivacy writes credential status and a proof hash to Canton via Daml. Raw personal data stays off-ledger.

Step 3

Reuse Across Apps

Any integrated Canton app can verify credential state and validity without requiring repeated KYC.

Why We Built It

Onboarding fragmentation creates repeated KYC costs, poor user experience, and unnecessary exposure of sensitive data.

For Users

Complete identity verification once, then carry a reusable credential across participating Canton apps.

For Builders

Add shared KYC to your app without building your own identity infrastructure or storing sensitive KYC data.
